About D Ber

Purveyor of Deep and Driving House & Techno

D Ber is a fixture in the underground club and festival circuit, known for his ability to blend the rhythmic pulse of Afro House with the driving force of Tech House and the emotive landscapes of Melodic Techno.

With notable performances at iconic venues and festivals, his sets are a journey—crafted not just to make people dance, but to create a shared, high-energy experience. D Ber is dedicated to the art of the mix, delivering a sound that is both sophisticated and relentlessly infectious.
